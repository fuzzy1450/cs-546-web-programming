Tips & Tricks!

  1. Play with your team!

    Your most powerful weapon is your fellow contestants. Treat your teammates like lighthouses, and let the flash of their gunfire protect you from the rocky shores of defeat.

  2. Go for the Revive!

    If a teammate is down, prioritize picking them back up. No option can tip the scales like a good revive.

  3. Punish the Revive!

    If you just got a pick, be sure to camp the poor sap's trophy. If their team is decent, their teammate will be by soon. Make them regret it.

  4. Goo is OP!

    Instant defense, versatile movement option, and it can even stop thieves. Goo will get the job done, whatever that job may be.

  5. Rhythm Wins Fights!

    Your opponent could be cheating, but even aimhacks wont help him shoot through your cover. Practice patience under fire, and only shoot when they need to reload.
